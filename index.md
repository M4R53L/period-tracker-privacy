# Privacy Policy — Period Tracker

**Last updated: July 14, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Period Tracker" mobile application (the "App") handles your information.

---

## In Short

**Period Tracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.** All information you enter is stored only on your device. There is no account, no server, and no cloud sync.

---

## Information We Collect

**None.** Period Tracker does not require an account, an email address, a phone number, or any other personal identifier. There is no sign-up or login. We do not collect any information about you.

## Information You Enter

The flow level, mood, symptoms, and notes you choose to log are stored **locally on your device** using Apple's on-device storage (SwiftData). This data never leaves your device, and we have no way to access it.

## Local Notifications

If you turn on reminders, Period Tracker schedules **local notifications** on your device for your predicted period, fertile window, and ovulation. These notifications are created and delivered entirely on your device. They do not involve any server and do not transmit any data off your device.

## Data Storage & Sync

All data is stored on your device. Period Tracker does **not** use any server, cloud service, or cloud sync. There is no backup stored anywhere other than your device.

## Third Parties

Period Tracker does **not** use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools. No third party receives any data from the App.

## Health Data

Period Tracker does **not** use HealthKit or any other health-data framework. No health data is read from or written to other apps.

## Data Sharing & Selling

We do **not** share, sell, or rent your data to anyone. There is no data to share — it all stays on your device.

## Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is kept on your device for as long as you use the App. **Deleting the App from your device permanently deletes all of your data.** There is no remote copy to recover.

## Children's Privacy

Period Tracker is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13. As described above, we do not collect data from anyone — including children.

## Your Choices

You can delete any logged day at any time from within the App, and you can turn reminders on or off in Settings. To erase all data at once, simply delete the App from your device.
